1) Summary

  • ✅ Defined the goal: a distraction-free note-taking device
  • ✅ Identified key features: e-ink display, small keyboard, minimal UI, no notifications
  • ✅ Compared to existing tools and identified gaps
  • ✅ Chose direction: hardware + software hybrid device

2) What I did

Planning

  • Defined the goal — Created a distraction-free note-taking device concept focused on deep work and minimal UI.
  • Identified key features — E-ink display for eye comfort, small keyboard, minimal UI with no notifications or distractions.
  • Competitive analysis — Compared to existing tools: iPad, laptop, notebooks. Identified gaps in market.

Research

  • Device direction — Decided on hardware + software hybrid approach combining custom electronics with thoughtful software design.

3) Decisions made

  • Decision: Build a hardware device rather than software-only solution.
    • Reason: Physical constraints force focus and intentionality. Unique experience impossible with existing hardware.
    • Tradeoff: Much higher complexity, longer timeline, requires 3D printing and electronics.

4) Issues / blockers

  • Blocker: Selecting the right screen size (10.3" vs 13.3").
    • What I tried: mocking up UI on paper.
    • Next attempt: cardboard mockups of physical dimensions.
