Squirtle Sprayer

A playful, functional 3D-printed figure that actually sprays water—combining circuits, pneumatics, and delight.

Problem

Bring a character to life with real motion and a surprising interaction, while keeping the system safe and maintainable.

Process

CAD for enclosure, control circuit planning, relay-driven pump actuation, 3D-printed parts and iterative assembly. Tested flow rate, sealing, and power safety.

Outcome

Working, durable prototype with reliable spray behavior and a playful reveal. Next steps: enclosure refinement and adding a capacitive touch trigger.

Tools

  • Raspberry Pi
  • 12V pump + relay
  • 3D Printing (CAD)
